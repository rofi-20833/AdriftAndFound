ADRIFT & FOUND

ADRIFT AND FOUND website is an online platform that allows individuals and institutions to report and claim lost items. It helps to keep track of lost items and reunite them with their rightful owners. The process is simple, users can log on to the website, fill out a form with information about the item and if someone finds it, they can search for the item and contact the person who reported it to arrange for the return. ADRIFT AND FOUND website can also be customized to fit the needs of the institution and can improve organization and efficiency.

About US

"Ladies and gentlemen, have you ever lost something important and had no idea where to start looking for it? Whether it's a laptop, a phone, or even a personal belonging, the feeling of loss can be overwhelming. But what if I told you that there's a solution that can help you recover those lost items? I present to you ADRIFT & FOUND. ADRIFT AND FOUND website is an online platform that allows individuals to report and claim lost items. The process is simple, all you have to do is log on to the website, fill out a form with information about the item, and voila, you've reported a lost item. Now, let's say someone finds the item, they can also log on to the website and search for the lost item using the information provided, and if it matches, they can contact the person who reported it and arrange for the item to be returned..
